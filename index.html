About

I'm an Applied Scientist at Microsoft, working on the Copilot Tuning team where I develop AI applications and agents that enhance Microsoft Copilot's capabilities. My focus is on customizing and fine-tuning AI agents using large language models (LLMs) and machine learning techniques to deliver tailored enterprise solutions.

Before joining Microsoft, I spent nearly 4 years at Uber as a Data Scientist and Senior Data Scientist, working with GenAI model applications and machine learning-based products. I hold a Master's degree in Production Engineering from UNESP, with published research in peer-reviewed journals, and I'm currently pursuing an MSc in Cybersecurity at the University of London.
